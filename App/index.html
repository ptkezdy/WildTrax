Our Story

For our submission to Wildhacks 2024, we present to
you: WildTrax. Wildtrax is an all-in-one system that 
lets you add your events and tasks to a calendar and
to-do list at the same time, letting users track events
and giving them adviceon what they should work. Our goal
with this project is to help everybusy person - whether
they're students, workers, teachers, or someone else -
make the most of the one resource we can't reclaim: time.
In doing so we hope to help better the world by making
everyone more productive.
